Allow me to introduce myself...

I am Marcos Bernal España. Ironically, you may guess from my last surname that you found another crazy Spaniard... well, you are not far from being right. Anyway, if you want to know more about me, I am finishing the Master 2 in Data Science in the Technical University of Madrid.

My interest are quite large and mainly focused, but not limited, on computer engineering. I like robotics, software developing (including videogames :P), security and networking. I like to learn something useful everyday and I do my best to achieve it.

If I had to sum up myself, I would say that I am a problem solving person. I really like easy problems and medium ones, but hard problems are not big deal since one can split them and have several small lovely problems instead!

When I am not working nor developing/researching projects by my own, I like to hang out with friends, play some videogames and do all kind of sports. Particularly, I love hockey and the one that I play is Field Hockey. Field hockey is usually misunderstood with the other variants of hockey i.e. Ice Hockey or Roller Hockey which are quite different.
